waiting hidden eyes

watching cast downwards

slowly falling into

oceans of water and

thousands dead all the

fish drowned in the

oil painted rocks and

rendered imagery found

wanting lacking something

which is nothing they

will tell you so

tell you lies and truths

lost in the now here

is the news where are

the cameras hidden

in the other cameras

you would never think

to look to question

the anchorman is

laughing at you in

the commercial break
