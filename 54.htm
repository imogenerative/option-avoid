unseeing vigil no

quarter asked nor given

clarity of thought now

ending upon us ending

drawing ever closer drawn

with blood over reluctant

pagination unconventional

now listen here listen

careful hunting the beast

rages with blood over

reluctant missionary

notices declining population

never answer the door

could be who we want it to

begin again forget the

mistakes the illusions

tired eyes and unblinking

sentinel the dry the sharp

at the back of your

mind a splinter in the

throat of the false

prophets on television
